Collecting Arcade Machines

Hello! I have a hobby that I love. Since 2002 I have been collecting arcade and pinball machines. I started my collection when i was living in an apartment. Unfortunately the space was to limited and I wanted to take my hobby seriously, so I had to move. Now I am living in a house with a 100m2 garage which means that I have room for a few more games than I had in my apartment.

Through the years I have been across the country and collected some nice machines, I have also been importing some machines from Japan and Italy
